# VERA Governance Evidence Deliverables (Illustrative)

This document describes the **governance evidence generated locally by a buyer**
when evaluating the VERA Commercial Bundle.

It is intended to make the evaluation outcomes tangible and auditable.
No evidence is transmitted to the vendor.

---

## A) Integrity & Provenance Evidence

**Cryptographic Checksum Log**  
Verification of the `.sha256` integrity anchor against the delivered evaluation ZIP.

**Version-Locked Manifest**  
A version-anchored identifier binding the evaluated governance logic to a unique artifact ID and audit tag.

---

## B) Evaluation Execution Evidence

**Deterministic Replay Script**  
The command-line evaluation tool used to verify identical results on a second machine (“Device-B”).

**Evaluation Execution Transcript**  
A timestamped, step-by-step record of each governance invariant verified during the local evaluation run.

---

## C) Governance Boundary Evidence

**Explicit Refusal Traces**  
Technical evidence showing where and how VERA deterministically refused disallowed actions during boundary testing.

**Constraint Mapping Report**  
Documentation mapping each enforced governance constraint to the corresponding verification test.

---

## D) Claims Substantiation

**VERA Substantiated Claims Certificate**  
A version-anchored document mapping evaluation results directly to the published governance claims.

---

## E) License & Reliance Evidence

**Version-Anchored Commercial License**  
Confirmation that reliance rights apply only to the specific evaluated artifact version.

**Governance Fidelity Attestation**  
A formal statement that the evaluated artifact version is unmodified and conforms to the VERA governance standard.

---

All evidence is generated locally by the evaluator.
No operational data, logs, or outputs are shared with the vendor.
